The Word Game Shooting Gallery

Let's Get Started!

Jumble Hunt is a shooting gallery style word jumble game. The letters of a word will be rearranged in a random order and displayed on the screen. The player must “shoot” the letters in the correct order to unscramble the word.

There are 5 levels to Jumble Hunt. Level 1 begins with four letter words, as you progress through the levels, a letter will be added to the length of the word. By Level 5, the words will be eight letters long.

To advance to the next level, you must correctly unscramble three words per level. You get three attempts to correctly unscramble a presented word. If you have three unsuccessful attempts during the game, you lose and the game is over. If you successfully unscramble three words per level with less than three failed attempts, you win the game and the game is over. You do have access to unlimited cheats, however for each cheat your score will be reset to 0.
